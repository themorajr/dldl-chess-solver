Soul Land: New World Puzzle Solver


How to Use

  • Select a grid: 3x3, 3x4, or 5x3 with empty spaces for no poles.
  • Click on the empty spaces until they turn black to mark them as poles.
  • Red indicates lights that are turned on.
  • Any orientation is fine, but placing it facing the grid front makes it easier to view.
